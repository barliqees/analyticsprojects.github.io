Xpress Tech Supply chain Analysis using Tableau.

Xpress Tech. is a start up company that just diversified into logistic and food delivery and are therefore keen on getting insight on deliveries based on customer preferences from a recent survey they conducted. After extensive data cleaning and calculations were performed on the data set, it was deduced that the vast majority of those that took part in the survey are singles, students, and individuals with low income with age ranging between 20-28. Also, some respondents face issues with payment gateway and mention the hassle related to refunds and surcharges e.t.c. Recommendations were given and it was projected if followed diligently, revenue will increase by 35% in 6 months.
